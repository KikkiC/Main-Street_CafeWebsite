- About Us -

Starting from 1998, we have been serving our creamy, house-made coffee for our customers. We offer some of the world’s finest fresh-roasted whole bean coffees along with freshly-baked goods daily. From the beginning, Main Street prioritizes the tradition of bringing customers a sense of connection. Our ultimate mission is to inspire and nurture the human spirit – one person, one cup, and one neighborhood at a time.
